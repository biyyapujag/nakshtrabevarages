The Nakshatra Story

Water is not just a necessity—it is an experience.

Driven by a deep passion for beverages and an uncompromising commitment to quality. Today, we stand proud as one of the premier, most advanced pure water plants in the entire Godavari districts, redefining premium hydration for our region.

Our Source & Unmatched Purity

Nakshatra Beverages is crafted with exceptional care. We begin with high-quality, pristine sources renowned for their excellent mineral balance. But we don't just stop at great sourcing; we prioritize absolute purity from the source directly to the bottle.

Untouched by Hand

Our state-of-the-art production process ensures zero human contact with the water, guaranteeing its completely untouched, natural essence.

Rigorous Standards

As a fully BIS-compliant plant, our facility operates under the watchful eyes of dedicated professionals who continuously monitor quality at every single stage of production.

This unwavering commitment to safety and technology is what truly sets Nakshatra Beverages apart as a trusted household name in the Godavari region.

Crafted for Every Occasion

Presented in elegantly designed bottles and cans, Nakshatra Beverages is designed to elevate your lifestyle. Whether you are seeking a personal moment of refreshment or looking for a sophisticated addition to your table, our premium water perfectly complements your desire for both quality and style.
